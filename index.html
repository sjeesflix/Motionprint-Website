Assessment methods

Built on validated standards

Motionprint Ergo supports established ergonomic methods and automates their scoring using motion capture data.

Available
KIM-LHC
Key Indicator Method — Lifting, Holding, Carrying

Widely used to assess physical workload during lifting, holding and carrying tasks. Motionprint Ergo automates KIM-LHC scoring using motion capture data.
