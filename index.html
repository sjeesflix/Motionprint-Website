How it works

Three steps to a
complete risk report

01

Upload your Xsens data

Export your recording from MVN as a .mvnx file and upload it directly into Motionprint Ergo. No complex setup or conversion required.

02

Automatic analysis

Define one or more task events in your recording, then let Motionprint Ergo calculate NIOSH or KIM-LHC scores and identify risk factors — automatically.

03

Export your report

Export a professional, standardised report as PDF or Excel with your own branding — structured for H&S documentation, client delivery or regulatory compliance review.

Supported methods

Built on validated
ergonomic standards

Two validated methods available now, with more continuously being added to the platform.

Available now
NIOSH
For evaluating manual lifting and lowering tasks

Calculates the Lifting Index (LI) for single-task lifting or the Composite Lifting Index (CLI) for multi-task scenarios — quantifying the physical demand of manual lifting operations.

Available now
KIM-LHC
For assessing lifting, holding and carrying tasks

German BAuA standard for evaluating the risk of physical overload from manual handling tasks — based on load weight, duration, posture and working conditions.
