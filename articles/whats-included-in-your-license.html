Your Motionprint Ergo license provides access to the full ergonomic assessment platform, tailored to the scale of your team. This article explains what each license tier includes, the fair use terms that apply to all plans, how to add seats, and what happens at renewal and upgrade.

1 What's Covered

2 Fair Use Terms

All Motionprint Ergo licenses are subject to fair use conditions that ensure the licensing system remains secure and that each seat represents a genuine user.

Device limits

Each seat entitles one user to activate Motionprint Ergo on up to 2 devices simultaneously — for example, a desktop workstation and a laptop. Activations are tracked by machine fingerprint; switching devices requires deactivating one before activating another.

Offline usage

Once activated, Motionprint Ergo can run without an internet connection for 30 days. After this period a 7-day grace period begins, during which the app continues to function but prompts re-authentication. If connectivity is not restored within the grace period, the app enters read-only mode until the license is re-validated.

Non-transferable

Licenses are issued to a named user and are non-transferable between individuals. Exceeding device limits or sharing a license key between users may result in suspension. Contact support to reassign a seat when a team member changes.

3 Adding Seats

Your seat count can be increased at any time during your subscription period. Additional seats are pro-rated to align with your existing renewal date.

How to add seats

  • Contact Motionprint support or your account manager to request additional seats — upgrades are processed the same business day
  • For API-managed deployments, additional seats can be requested via the license server API; the generate_license.py script supports specifying updated seat counts when issuing a revised key

Seat upgrades

When seats are added, a new license key is issued with the updated seat limit. Existing activations continue without interruption — users simply re-activate with the new key the next time the license is checked. No data or settings are lost during a seat upgrade.

Tier changes

Moving from Individual to Team, or from Team to Enterprise, constitutes a tier upgrade rather than a simple seat addition. Contact support to receive a custom quote for your organisation's needs.

4 Renewal & Upgrades

Motionprint Ergo licenses are annual subscriptions. Renewal reminders are sent automatically so your team is never caught off guard by an expiring license.

Renewal timeline

30 days before expiry
First renewal notification sent by email. Full platform access continues uninterrupted.
7 days before expiry
Follow-up reminder sent. Urgent renewal prompt displayed in the app.
Expiry date
License expires. A 7-day read-only grace period begins — you can view and export existing reports but cannot run new assessments.
After grace period
Full lock. The application requires a renewed license key before any functionality is restored.

Upgrading your plan

Upgrades to a higher tier — for example from Team to Enterprise — can be requested at any time by contacting support. Enterprise and Academic tiers require a custom quote based on seat count and organisational requirements. Upgrades take effect immediately upon payment; a new license key is issued for the upgraded plan.
